Overachievement Kicker

  • Only triggers when revenue exceeds 120% of the aggregate monthly target.
  • Rate: 0.75% of revenue beyond the 120% threshold.
  • Uncapped — the more you exceed, the more you earn.

Calculation Flow

Step 1: Base Incentive = revenue × slab rate
Step 2: Input Bonus = base × (20% | 10% | 0%)
Step 3: Kicker = (revenue − target × 1.2) × 0.75% (only if >120%)
Step 4: Penalty A = base × missed-connect rate
Step 5: Penalty B = shortfall-multiplier × shortfall × slab rate
Step 6: Subtotal = base + input + kicker − penA − penB
Step 7: Penalty C = subtotal × TL-failure rate
Step 8: Penalty D = (subtotal − penC) × closer-failure rate
Final:  Max(0, subtotal − penC − penD)
